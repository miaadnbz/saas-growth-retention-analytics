## SaaS Growth Analytics

**Question:** Which channels acquire customers who activate, convert, and remain subscribed?

Built a customer-level analytics layer joining 12,000 synthetic SaaS accounts with marketing touches, product events, subscriptions, invoices, campaign spend, and support data. Modelled the pipeline in BigQuery and dbt, documented governed KPI definitions, and designed a four-page Looker Studio decision dashboard.

**Finding:** Activated paid accounts achieved 86.4% 90-day retention versus 65.2% among non-activated accounts. Referral showed the strongest observed revenue efficiency, while Paid Social offered the largest onboarding opportunity.

**Recommendation:** Test a day-3 milestone intervention, report retained CAC, and reallocate paid budget only after incrementality is validated.

**Tools:** SQL · dbt · BigQuery · Python · Looker Studio · GitHub Actions
